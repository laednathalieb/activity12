Bachelor in Applied Computing Technology

BACT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computinng. The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web mobile development and network system administration. Futhermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is devided from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No.31. series of 2021.

Program Objects

The Program prepares learners with the practical skills in multimedia technology. web mobile development, and network/systems administration, and supervisorial skills to manage an organization in these areas of competencies, engage in a startup business or puruse a teaching career.

Specific Objectivs:

The learner should be able to:

1. Demonstate understanding of the importance of being an computing professional whemn making work-related decisions.

2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network systems, and developmentof productivity tools.

3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leardership roles when appropriate.

4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.

5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the Program.

6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities.
